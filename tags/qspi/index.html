Zynq-7000 QSPI Clock Generation

2023-01-05 ::
#Zynq-7000  #FPGA  #Kernel  #QSPI 
Introduction This article, we will explore how to configure the QSPI controller clocks on Zynq-7000. It provide a brief overview of the QSPI clock generation block and also describes the how to configure the clock frequency in UBoot and Linux kernel. Clock Generation Hardware The QSPI Controller on the Zynq is driven by a QSPI_REF_CLK. The QSPI_REF_CLK can be sourced from either ARM, DDR and IO PLL Blocks. After selecting the, clock source, the clock is further divided using a 6-bit divisor.
